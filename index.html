Biography

My research bridges the studies on finance and healthcare, two crucial academic fields in the broad economic literature. The connection is through classical decisions in corporate finance: financing and investment. I use novel datasets on granular operation and innovation information in the healthcare industry and apply empirical and theoretical methods from finance to study these issues. My research focuses on financing U.S. hospital industries and innovation investments in the healthcare industry.
