Biography

My research bridges the studies on finance and healthcare, which are two crucial academic fields in the broad economic literature. The connection is through classical decisions in corporate finance: financing and investment. I utilize novel datasets on granular operation and innovation information in the healthcare industry and apply empirical and theoretical methods from finance to study these issues.
