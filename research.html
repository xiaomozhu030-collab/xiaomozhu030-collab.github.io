Publications

  • 1. Find and Replace: R&D Investment Following the Erosion of Existing Products
    Joshua L. Krieger, Xuelin Li, and Richard T. Thakor
    Management Science, 68(9), 6552-6571 (September 2022)

    This paper examines how firms adjust their R&D investment strategies when facing the erosion of existing products, providing insights into the dynamics of innovation in competitive markets.

  • 2. Common Ownership and Innovation Efficiency
    Xuelin Li, Tong Liu, and Lucian A. Taylor
    Journal of Financial Economics, Volume 147, Issue 3, Pages 475-497 (March 2023)

    This study investigates the effects of common ownership on innovation efficiency, focusing on the interaction between ownership structure and innovation outcomes in competitive industries.

    [PDF] [Online Abstract]
  • 3. Merchants of Death: The Effect of Credit Supply Shocks on Hospital Outcomes
    Cyrus Aghamolla, Pinar Karaca-Mandic, Xuelin Li, and Richard T. Thakor
    American Economic Review, Vol. 114, No. 11, pp. 3623–68 (November 2024)

    We study the effect of credit supply shocks on hospital outcomes, with implications for healthcare financing during economic downturns. This paper explores the risk and return dynamics in the healthcare industry.

    [PDF] [Online Abstract]
  • 4. Financial Effects of Remote Product Delivery: Evidence from Hospitals
    Kimberly Cornaggia, Xuelin Li, and Zihan Ye
    Review of Financial Studies, Volume 37, Issue 9, Pages 2817-2854 (September 2024)

    In this paper, we analyze the financial impact of remote product delivery on hospitals, examining how external factors such as capital structure affect the adoption of remote healthcare technologies.
